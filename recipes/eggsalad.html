Egg Salad Recipe

Description

This egg salad tastes wonderful in a sandwich and will definitely be devoured! It's really good on rye. Make deli-worthy egg salad sandwiches at home with this top-rated egg salad recipe! You'll come back to it again and again.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Green onion
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil, peel, and chop the eggs.
  2. Combine the chopped eggs and the remaining ingredients.
  3. Serve on bread or with crackers.
